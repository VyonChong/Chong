  1.   About Our BookStore.

    The goal of this bookstore is to provide user and/or customer a virtual bookstore experience by allowing you to browse, search, as well a purchase book through digital way without the need to enter a physical store. Our bookstore has a large variety of book selection for all type of reader ranging from novel, comic, to educational material with different genre.


  2.   How to find a particular book?

    You can locate a specific book by key-in the book's name in the search bar on top of the page, then either press "enter" or click the list of book shown after key-in the book's name.


  3.   Pricing of book in our store.

    The pricing of books listed in our bookstore is based on the market price.


  4.   Promotion.

    Our bookstore run different promotion from time to time. Some of our promotion is only exclusive for membership holder. If you wan't to know more about any future promotion that we will run, you can kindly follow our social media for latest new.


  5.   BookStore Account.

    To own an affiliate account, click the "Login" on the top right of the page. Then, click "Sign up" below to register a new account with the required information.


  6.   Membership.

    If you are interested in becoming a membership holder of our bookstore, you need to have an registered account in order to purchase a monthly subsricption. A membership holder can enjoy special promotion provided us.


  7.   Ways of Delivery.

    All books purchased will be delivered to your doorstep via our courier partner.


  8.   Delivery Time.

    All order will be delivered from 2 - 7 days depending on the receiver's location. You can kindly check in with our courier partner for more accurate time.


  9.   Parcel Status.

    Customer who ordered our product will receive a tracking number provided by our courier partner in your E-mail. You can use the tracking number to track where is your order through our courier partner.


  10.   Refund of Product Purchased.

    Our store will not refund any purchased made


  11.   Refund of Damaged Product upon Delivered.

    If a product of our is damaged when delivered. Please take a video of it as a evidence an contact our customer service for refund of product.


  12.   Product not Delivered on Time.

    Please allow extra 1 - 2 days for the product to be delivered. If product is still not delivered, do contact us.


  13.   Received Wrong Product.

    Please contact our customer service for further action.


  14.   Unavailable During Time of Delivery.

    You can contact to our courier partner with the order ID to comfirm the location of your parcel and further action required.


  15.   Placing an Order.

    Step 1: Find the books you interested.
    Step 2: Click the "Add to Cart" button to add the book to the Cart page.
    Step 3: After adding all the book wanted. Click the "Cart" on the top right of the page.
    Step 4: Double check all the item you wanted to purchase.
    Step 5: Click the "Checkout" button.
    Step 6: Fill in your E-mail, Name, Phone Number, and Address.
    Step 7: Enter your payment details and click "Process with Payment" to comfirm your order.


  16.   Order Comfirmation.

    After successfully placing an order, you will receive an E-mail to comfirm your purchases along with the order details and a tracking number to track your parcel.


  17.   Forgot Account ID / Password.

    You can click the "LOGIN" on the top right of the web page. After that, click the highlighted link "Forgot Password?" and fill in any required information after which you will receive a new password in your E-mail or Phone. Another way to recover an account is to contact our customer service.


  18.   Changes to Account Information.

    Log in to ur affiliate account. In "Your Profile" tab, click "Edit Profile" to change any personal information and click comfirm to update your account information to the latest. Make sure the new information is real.
